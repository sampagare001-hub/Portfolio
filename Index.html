About

A Voice Built on

Shamshon Pagare · Mumbai

I'm a content writer, essayist, and creative storyteller from Mumbai with over six years of writing professionally across formats, languages, and industries.

My work lives in the space between intellect and feeling — longform essays that argue and move, scripts that breathe, poems that linger. I write in English and Marathi, and I bring the same rigour to a brand brief as I do to a personal essay.

Whether it's cultural commentary, a short film dialogue, a corporate narrative, or a shayari that aches — I write it with a distinct voice that clients remember.
